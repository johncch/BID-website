Introducing ZipGig

ZipGig is designed to get you a helping hand for a quick task fast so that business never slows down. The tasks could be any piece of work like helping man the grocery store to helping in getting goods to a customer.

The home screen has two basic tabs allowing the user to either act as a helper or the task assigner. The helper tab allows a user to view current available tasks in the locality and take them up if he/she wishes to. If the task is taken up, the application sends a notification to the task assigner for him to either accept or decline the helper's interest in the same.

Within a few minutes, if the helper's offer to help is accepted, he/she gets a notification on his iPhone and can now either view the directions to reach the task address or contact the task assigner to get more details.

Once the task is completed, the assigner pays the helper through a secure connection quite easily via ZipGig itself. The assigner thus gets his work done without having to worry about getting a helping hand, while the helper earns some money through a quick task in his locality.
